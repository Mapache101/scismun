Letter from the Secretary-General

Honorable Delegates, Respected Directors, and Distinguished Guests,

It is with immense pleasure and profound honor that I welcome you to the annual Santa Cruz International School Model United Nations (SCISMUN). In an era defined by rapid change and complex global challenges, the need for diplomatic dialogue and innovative problem-solving has never been greater.

This year's theme focuses on sustainable development and global cooperation. We have meticulously crafted committees that will challenge you to think critically, negotiate passionately, and draft resolutions that reflect a deep understanding of international relations.
