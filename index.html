¿Qué es SCISMUN?

Es una simulación académica y educativa de las Naciones Unidas que brinda a los participantes la oportunidad de sumergirse en el mundo de la diplomacia, la política internacional y la resolución de conflictos.

El objetivo principal de un MUN es promover el entendimiento de los desafíos mundiales y la importancia de la diplomacia como medio para abordarlos. Además, brinda a los participantes la oportunidad de conocer a personas con intereses similares, construir amistades y establecer redes profesionales que pueden durar toda la vida.

2026 Conference Theme

Beyond Borders

“Beyond Borders” is the theme of this year’s SCISMUN conference. Its predecessor, “Voices of Our Youth,” highlighted the power of dialogue and peaceful negotiation over violence and aggression in addressing global challenges. This year, we build upon that foundation.

The world we live in has evolved—and so have the challenges we face. “Beyond Borders” recognizes this shift toward a more interconnected, modern, and technological era. It calls on us to move past divisions, to think collaboratively, and to build bridges that lead to meaningful, global solutions.

Welcome Address

Letter from the Secretary-General

Welcome to SCISMUN, the Model United Nations of Santa Cruz International School.

It is an honor to serve as President of a community that brings together students who are not only passionate about global affairs, but also committed to understanding the perspectives behind them. At SCISMUN, we believe that diplomacy is not just about representing a country it is about learning how to listen, to question, and to work collaboratively toward meaningful solutions.

In today's world, challenges such as climate change, conflict, and inequality remind us that no nation stands alone. Through debate and dialogue, our delegates step into the roles of global leaders, tackling complex issues while developing the skills of negotiation, critical thinking, and respectful communication.

But beyond policy and procedure, SCISMUN is also about people. It is about creating a space where students feel empowered to share their ideas, challenge assumptions, and grow into confident, globally minded individuals. Whether you are joining us for the first time or returning with experience, your voice matters here.

As you explore our website and learn more about our conferences and initiatives, I invite you to see SCISMUN not just as an academic activity, but as a platform for youth leadership and global engagement. Together, we are not only simulating diplomacy we are shaping the mindset of the next generation of leaders.

Jose Ernesto Loayza

Secretary-General, SCISMUN
